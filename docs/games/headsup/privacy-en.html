Privacy Policy — Heads UP

Last updated: June 11, 2024

1. Overview

Heads UP is a party game developed by Joaquim Tito. This policy explains how the app handles data on iOS and Android devices.

2. Data we collect

  • Analytics and crash reports. We use Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics to understand usage patterns and fix issues. These tools collect anonymized events (e.g., feature usage, app version, device model) and crash metadata. We do not store personally identifiable information.
  • Advertising. The app uses Google AdMob to serve banner and interstitial ads. AdMob may collect identifiers (such as the Advertising ID) to provide and measure ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in the device settings.

3. How we use data

Data is used solely to monitor performance, fix bugs, and keep the game sustainable through ads. We do not sell or share data with third parties beyond our analytics and ad providers.

4. Your choices

  • Disable personalized ads in the system privacy settings.
  • Delete the app to stop any further data collection. Existing analytics logs in Firebase automatically expire according to Google’s retention policies.

5. Children’s privacy

Heads UP is designed for general audiences but is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child provided us information, contact us so we can remove it.

6. Contact

For privacy questions, email joacatito@gmail.com.

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
